FAQ

What advisors ask first.

Do you pay referral commissions?

No. We've designed the program to work cleanly under the regulatory frameworks that apply to tax advisors, auditors, and other regulated advisor professions in the EU. The value is in the workflow, the reporting, and the access to our team, not in commissions.

How do you handle client confidentiality?

Each referred client signs their own agreement with HansePay. We do not share client information back to the referring advisor unless the client explicitly consents. When they do, you receive monthly reporting and standard format exports.

Will you try to sell my clients other services?

No. HansePay does FX, cross-border payments, and EMT issuance. We do not sell tax, accounting, audit, or consulting services. Your client relationship stays where it is.

How fast does a referred client get onboarded?

Most referred clients are operational within two weeks. Advisor-introduced clients get a dedicated onboarding contact and faster KYC processing than self-service signups.

What kind of clients should I refer?

Mid-market businesses with cross-border activity. Typical triggers include foreign subsidiaries, international suppliers, cross-border invoicing, or import-export operations. Annual FX volume of around 1 million EUR or more is where the value becomes obvious.

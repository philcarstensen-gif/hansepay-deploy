FAQ

What partners ask first.

How is this different from the Embed Payments program?

Embed Payments is for software platforms that integrate HansePay deeply through API into their product. Resell is for networks, institutions, and resellers who bring HansePay to their audience commercially, with light or no technical integration. Many partners start with Resell and grow into Embed when deeper integration becomes valuable.

Is full white-label available today?

Not yet. Today we offer HansePay-branded reselling and co-branded partnerships ("powered by HansePay"). Full white-label, where only the partner brand is visible, is on our roadmap. If white-label is critical for your model, tell us about your timing in the conversation and we'll be honest about where we are.

What commercial terms do you offer?

Commercial terms are shaped individually right now. Margin, revenue share, and tiered structures are all on the table. Because the program is in its early phase, our first partners help define what works and lock in terms before structures harden.

Who contracts the end customer?

In current setups, HansePay contracts the end customer directly because HansePay is the regulated entity. Your role is to introduce, position, and support. With future white-label, the contracting model will adapt.

How quickly can we go live?

A co-branded referral path can be live within weeks. A deeper co-brand with custom onboarding takes longer. We move fast with first partners and the timeline depends on the model and your audience size.

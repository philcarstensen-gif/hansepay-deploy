FAQ

Common questions

Is HansePay regulated?

Yes. HansePay operates under Atrya Technologies SIA, a BaFin-licensed e-money institution regulated under EU Payment Services Directive 2 (PSD2). Client funds are held in segregated accounts and are never used for operational purposes.

How does zero-markup FX actually work?

We source exchange rates directly from interbank liquidity providers and pass them through to you without adding a spread. Instead, we charge a fixed transaction fee shown clearly before you confirm. You always know exactly what the payment costs before you send.

How long do transfers take?

Most EUR SEPA transfers settle same-day. Cross-border SWIFT payments typically settle within 1–2 hours for major corridors (USD, GBP, CHF, JPY). Exotic corridors may take one business day. Every transfer is traceable in real time from the moment it's confirmed.

What currencies do you support?

35+ currencies including USD, GBP, CHF, JPY, CNY, AED, BRL, INR, SEK, NOK, DKK, PLN and more. For institutional volumes and less-common corridors, custom arrangements are available — speak with one of our FX dealers.

Is there a minimum transfer amount?

No minimum. From a few hundred euros to multi-million-euro trades — same mid-market rate, same service. No volume tiers, no lock-in contracts. You pay per transaction and stop whenever you want.

FAQ

Common questions.

How fast are transfers?
Most transfers on major corridors (EUR/USD, EUR/GBP, EUR/CHF) settle same day. Transfers to Asia and the Middle East typically settle next business day. Your dedicated FX expert confirms exact timings when you book.
What fees does HansePay charge?
A single transparent transaction fee of 0.3–0.5%, depending on volume and currency pair. Zero markup on the exchange rate — you always receive the real mid-market rate. No monthly fees, no hidden charges, no minimum commitment.
Which currencies and countries do you support?
35+ currencies across 120+ countries — including USD, GBP, CHF, JPY, SGD, AED, CNY, AUD, CAD, and all major European currencies. Need a specific corridor not listed? Get in touch — we can often arrange it on request.
Is HansePay regulated and safe?
Yes. HansePay is a BaFin-regulated EU E-Money Institution. Client funds are held in fully segregated accounts. We comply with all applicable AML, KYC, and MiCAR regulations and are subject to regular audits under German and EU financial law.
How do I get started?
Open an account online in under 10 minutes — fully digital, no paperwork, no bank appointments. Digital ID verification is included. Once approved, your dedicated FX expert contacts you to walk through the platform and discuss your requirements.
Is there a minimum transfer amount?
The minimum transfer is EUR 1,000 (or equivalent). There is no maximum. For large or complex transfers, your FX expert can arrange tailored rates and bespoke settlement options — just get in touch.
